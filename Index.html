Welcome to the Course

This is a complete, self-paced undergraduate course in Abstract Algebra II presented in clean, easy-to-read HTML pages.

Whether you're a student looking for supplementary material, a self-learner, or just curious about modern mathematics, this course will guide you through the beautiful structures that underpin much of mathematics and computer science.

Course Overview

  • Part 1: Group Theory — Definitions, subgroups, homomorphisms, quotient groups, Sylow theorems, and classification of finite abelian groups.
  • Part 2: Ring Theory — Rings, ideals, integral domains, polynomial rings, and unique factorization.
  • Part 3: Field Theory & Galois Theory — Field extensions, splitting fields, and the fundamentals of Galois theory.
